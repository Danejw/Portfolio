Particle Powers

Ever wished you had superpowers?

Particle Powers is a small minimum viable product game where I've created a variety of effects applied as superpowers for a Virtual Reality player. Pick your favorite and survive the wave of enemies before the time runs out or death.

Firepower
Charge Blast
Flick Shot
Slow Down Time
Force Punch
Explosions

Particle Powers was my first Virtual Reality game where I played with particles and other visual effects to create a variety of superpowers. It was a blast to make because who doesn't want to play with superpowers!
